Aan is accidentaly become a siscon

He is an usual second grade of high-school student that living as independent in a house-rent since he come to this city a year ago.

That afternoon he has been back to his home after a tired day of studying in the school and unexpectedly a little cute girl came to his home then confess as his little sister.

Due to economy circumstance, he applying as part-time worker on a branch minimarket and found out cute junior-girl in his school as his jobshift-partner.

While he is on the way to buy a cosplay-suit as his 'little sister' asking and fell in unfortunate moment with the shopkeeper which is a little devil-cute girl.

These relationship begin with unexpected moment as usual romance-comedy story that always happened since his 'little sister' asking.

But how will they be?
